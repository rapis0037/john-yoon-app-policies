### 2. 앱 동작 방식

뮤직 테일즈 캔버스는 **완전한 오프라인 앱**입니다.

- 손가락 드로잉 데이터는 앱 실행 중에만 메모리에 유지되며, 앱 종료 시 자동 삭제됩니다.
- 도형 인식 및 음악 재생은 기기 내부에서만 처리됩니다.
- 인터넷 연결이 필요하지 않으며, 외부 서버와 통신하지 않습니다.
- 모든 사운드 파일은 앱에 포함되어 있습니다.

## English

### Privacy Policy

**Music Tales Canvas** is an educational app for infants and young children, developed by Cheese Tabby Entertainment ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects (and what it does not collect) and how it is handled.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

**Music Tales Canvas does not collect any personal information.**

The following types of information are never collected, stored, or transmitted:

- Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data (GPS, IP address, etc.)
- Device unique identifiers
- Usage statistics or analytics data
- Advertising identifiers
- Data obtained through sensitive permissions (microphone, camera, etc.)

---

### 2. How the App Works

Music Tales Canvas operates **entirely offline.**

- Finger-drawing data is held in memory only while the app is running and is automatically discarded when the app is closed.
- Shape recognition and music playback are processed entirely on the device.
- No internet connection is required, and the app does not communicate with any external servers.
- All sound files are bundled within the app.

---

### 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Music Tales Canvas is designed primarily for children aged 3–8.

- We do **not** collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13.
- There are no advertisements in the app.
- There are no in-app purchases.
- There are no social features, chat functions, or external links.

This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable laws in the Republic of Korea regarding the protection of children's personal information.

---

### 4. Device Permissions

Permissions requested by this app:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| None | Not applicable |

The app does not request any sensitive permissions, including internet access, microphone, camera, storage, or location.

---

### 5. Third-Party Services

Music Tales Canvas does not include:

- Advertising networks
- Analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
- Social media integrations
- External payment services

---

### 6. Data Security

Because no personal information is collected, there is no data to be transmitted or stored externally. All processing occurs solely on the user's device.

---

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be announced via the app store update notes or this page. For significant changes, an in-app notice will be posted.
