# Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 2026-05-07

This page explains how a user can request deletion of their account and related data for **Music Tales Kids Beta**.

This document applies to the Android app distributed under the package name **`com.johnyoon.musictaleskids.test`**.

## Developer Information

- Developer: `John Yoon`
- Contact email: `dygksdl12311@gmail.com`

## How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and related server-side data, send an email to:

- `dygksdl12311@gmail.com`

Please include the following in your request:

- the email address or login ID used for the app account
- the app name: `Music Tales Kids Beta`
- a short statement that you want your account deleted

Example subject line:

- `Account deletion request - Music Tales Kids Beta`

## What Will Be Deleted

After a valid deletion request is confirmed, we will delete the app account and associated server-side data that is reasonably linked to that account, including:

- account profile information
- child profile information
- gameplay progress data
- coin and reward data
- character selection and related saved state

## What May Remain on Your Device

Some data may remain on your device until you remove it yourself, including:

- locally stored login/session data on the device
- locally saved recording files created by the app
- cached app data

You can remove this local data by uninstalling the app and deleting any locally saved recordings from the device where applicable.

## Data That May Be Retained for a Limited Time

We may retain limited data for a reasonable period when necessary for:

- security and fraud prevention
- legal compliance
- dispute resolution
- enforcement of legal rights

If any data must be retained for these reasons, it will be kept only as long as reasonably necessary for that purpose.

## Important Notes

- Temporary deactivation or disabling is not treated as account deletion.
- If we need additional information to verify the request, we may respond by email before processing deletion.
